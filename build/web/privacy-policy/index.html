Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 June 2026

Introduction

Spiral Learning Intelligence System (SLIS) is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, teachers, schools, and visitors.

Information We Collect

  • Student name
  • Parent name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Grade and school information
  • Learning progress and assessment results
  • Messages submitted through forms
  • Device and browser information

How We Use Information

  • Provide educational services
  • Deliver learning content
  • Track academic progress
  • Send educational notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform performance
  • Communicate with parents and students

WhatsApp Communication

SLIS may send educational notifications, account updates, reminders, and support messages through WhatsApp Cloud API.

Data Security

All reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect user information.

Third Party Services

The platform may use:

  • Meta (Facebook & WhatsApp)
  • Google services
  • Railway hosting
  • PostgreSQL databases

Children's Privacy

Student accounts are managed under parental or school supervision where applicable.

User Rights

Users may request:

  • Access to their data
  • Correction of information
  • Deletion of information
  • Account closure
